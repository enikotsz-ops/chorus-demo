How the ritual works

No feed. No scroll. No algorithm. Just a question, an answer, and a moment you share.

Ask

Every member of the group gets the same question, from a curated theme bank.

Answer

You each record a voice answer, up to three minutes. Nobody can hear yours — or anyone else's — yet.

Unlock

Once everyone's answered, the round unlocks and the whole group listens together.
